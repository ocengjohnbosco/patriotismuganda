Janet Kataaha Museveni

Names: Janet Kataaha Museveni

Date of Birth: 24th June, 1948

Place of Birth: Ntungamo District

Marital Status: Married to Mr. Yoweri Kaguta Museveni in 1973.

Number of Children: They have 4 children and 10 grandchildren

Profession: Educationist

Academic: Bachelor of Education, Makerere University Diploma in Early Childhood Development from Sweden

Occupation Supportive role to the president

Member of Parliament for Ruhaama County Ntungamo District.

Involved in the following organizations which she initiated:
• Uganda Women’s Effort to Save Orphans (UWESO).
• The National Strategy for Advancement of Rural Women in Uganda (NSARWU)
• The Uganda Youth Forum to sensitize the youth against the killer disease HIV/AIDS

Hon. Janet Museveni is Patron to many social services initiatives that work towards reducing the suffering of the disadvantaged members of our community especially women and children. The list includes, safe Motherhood, Girl Guides, Burns and Plastic Surgery Institute at Mulago Hospital and CURE Children’s Hospital among others.

Hon. Mrs Museveni has over the years addressed many national and international conferences in areas of her work which includes social services for rural communities.
