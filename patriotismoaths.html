NATIONAL PATRIOTISM CORPS-UGANDA OATHS

NPC Teachers’ oath
I, …………….(Full names)…………shall;

• Be true to the mission to bring out the best spirit of Patriotism in each of my students.

• Be exemplary in the discharge of my duties and responsibilities.

• Guide my students to be good and. useful citizens of Uganda.

• Continue to learn and pass on the love of learning to students.

• Win the trust, support and co-operation of parents and community so as to enable me to achieve our mission.
FOR GOD AND MY COUNTRY



NPC Officers’ Creed.

I,………..(full names…), being an Officer of the NPC-U,
Promise To,

• Always be proud of the National Patriotism Corps- Uganda

• Be loyal to my country and respect those under whom I serve

• Ensure that the National Patriotism Corps is the leading Students’ Uniformed Corps in Uganda

• Be the present and future of distinguished patriots

• Honor those that have passed before me, and those to come

• Do nothing to disgrace my uniform or country

• hold it as my duty to be disciplined at all times so that I am an example to those I lead

• Maximize the potential of those under my charge through acquiring & imparting knowledge and skills

• Preserve and uphold the ideals of patriotism

• Always work hard and make the people of Uganda and Africa proud of the service I render

• Overcome all odds and uphold the dignity of my country.
FOR GOD AND MY COUNTRY



NPC INSRUCTORS, OATH

I,…….(full names)…………., being an instructor of the NPC- U,
Swear To,

• Always love and be loyal to Uganda

• Abide by the code of conduct of the NPC- U so as to be an example to those I train

• Maintain the highest standards of discipline in both my public and private conduct

• Carry out my duties with dedication and to the best of my ability in order to bring out the best in my trainees

• Promote harmony and team work among fellow instructors and trainees

• Always work hard and make the people of Uganda and Africa proud of the service I render

• Overcome all odds and uphold the dignity of my country.

FOR GOD AND MY COUNTRY



NPC NCOs’ OATH

I, (full name)…….. being an NCO of the National Patriotism Corps-Uganda, shall, at all times;

• Serve with pride and dedication.

• Excel in my Studies & NCO duties,

• Ensure the discipline of my……..(Section/Syndicate/Unit)

• Nurture members of my……..(Section/Syndicate/Unit) to their full potential

• Overcome all difficulties with singular resolve & determination

• Uphold the dignity of my country & defend the sovereignty of its people
FOR GOD AND MY COUNTRY



NPC Pledge

We, the members of the National Patriotism Corps of the Republic of Uganda, do hereby pledge our;

• Love and loyalty to Uganda

• Readiness to sacrifice for & serve our beloved country Uganda

• Resolve to maintain the highest standard of discipline and performance in the service of our motherland.
